    When will I get my airtime reward?

    Airtime is disbursed on a Monday

    How much is my airtime reward?

    R5

    My answer was not presented as an option. What does this mean?

    There is a discussion box below every challenge. Send us a message if you don’t think the correct answer has been displayed. Tell us what you think the answer should be and we’ll look into it for you.

    I chose the right answer but it says I got it wrong.

    There is a discussion box below every challenge. Send us a message saying that you think that the answer is incorrect, what you think the answer should be, and we will look into it for you

    Can my friend join?

    Not at the moment, but we will be opening up more spaces soon.

    Why are there only 3 questions per day?

    Because consistent practice throughout the year is the best way to revise maths (and we want to make it manageable as we know you have a lot of other homework)

    What if I miss a day?

    That's ok, you can answer the challenges you missed the next day. But the system is reset on a Sunday, so answer all outstanding questions on Saturday.

    I forgot my password. What do I do?

    Below the sign in button, there is a “Forgot Password” link. Click this and we will send you a new password

    I changed my phone number. What do I do?

    Below the sign in button, there is a “ProMaths learner but can’t Sign in” link. Click this and send us a message telling us your phone number has changed and we’ll register your new number for you.
